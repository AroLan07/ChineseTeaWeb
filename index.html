Six Major Types of Tea

In today's world, people typically use the "six major types of Chinese tea" concept to distinguish and purchase tea. However, in the past, tea lovers and merchants in China preferred to categorize these herbal leaves by their origin and production methods. Still, the modern classification is the most common and easiest to understand.
